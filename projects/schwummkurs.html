schwümmkurs

spring 2021
Postcard illustrations

Concept

Being alone... often has a negative connotation in our minds. But what if you consciously want to spend time alone? When you enjoy it and have fun spending time with yourself? That's exactly what my postcard series is about. This joy and the great moments that you can experience in your free time or hobby.

process

At first, it was just the sketches, which I then traced in Adobe Illustrator. I worked with a lot of color, which I reduced towards the end to give the postcards a more graphic look.
