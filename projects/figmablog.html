Blog redesign in Figma

AUTUMN 2022
Web design

Process

I started with some sketches but went quite fast into Figma to create some low-fidelity wireframes. The challenge was to create a responsive design for every device.

The design changed quite quickly and I decided on a card layout. this way the page looks tidy and clean. I tried different colors and font combinations but finally decided against the monochrome designs due to accessibility.

Final design

This is my final design. Try it out in the Figma prototype on the right. I have worked with many colors for the tags in order to quickly visualize a grouping of the blog posts for the user.
