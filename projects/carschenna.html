carschenna

AUTUMN 2021
Self-experiment

Concept

Does reduction make you happier?
What happens when I am alone for 5 days?
How will I manage without electricity?
Do I have the confidence?

I am clearly an extrovert and I get energy when I spend time with people I like, but I rarely do things alone. Somehow it's too boring for me or it makes me a bit sad at times when I can't share the experience with someone else. That's why I'm going to do this experiment. On the one hand, I'm reducing my contact with other people, and on the other hand, I'm going to live in a completely different environment and under different circumstances for the next five days. I'm moving into my grandmother's mountain hut, where there is no electricity and running water in the house. It's in Carschenna, near Chur. In any case, I am curious. Let the adventure begin.

I took all the photographs in this book documentation with my analog camera.
