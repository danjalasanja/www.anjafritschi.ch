sound of socks

AUTUMN 2021
INTERACTIVE installation

Concept

These sock puppets dance and sing to their hearts' desire. Just clap loudly once and they will start entertaining you.

process

Like with every other project, I started with a sketch. This is what my stage should look like.

Then it was time to design the puppets.

Now the stage.

I realized this project with the help of Arduino. I was able to control the mouths of the sock puppets with simple servo motors.
