Experimenting with Matter
‍
Groupwork By
Tanya, Anja, Tara, Irina

process

This whole project is based on the method of trial and error. It was all about experiencing matter.
We melted the plastic foil into different organic shapes with the laser cutter. In the second phase, we filled these with different materials and observed how the matter behaved.
