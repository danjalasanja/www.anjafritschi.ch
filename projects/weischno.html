weisch no?

winter 2022
interactive shirt print

Concept

This work is on the theme of "out of focus".
Everything revolves around memories that we want to hold on to. However, they fade more and more until they sometimes disappear completely. I have created graphic prints of my memories, which I can scan using AR and thus get back to the original memories. The whole thing is based on photos and postcards that I have collected over the years.
With this concept, you can carry your memories close to you every day and remember them.

process

misty, diffuse, veiled, faded... The brainstorming on the subject of blurred began. Blurred has many different facets, which made it difficult for me to choose a direction. I kept coming back to blurred memories. Often I see a picture in my photo book, but can only remember it because of the picture or thanks to stories.

I used the scanner to digitalize the pieces of memories. In the process, I tried to create shapes that were as interesting as possible. Rotations of the pictures usually looked weird, so I decided to use wave-like movements.
