short movie

Groupwork By
Tanya, Anja, Laberi, Elias

concept

With this short movie, we wanted to show how our phones can be different personalities of us. We feed it so much data that it almost knows us better and determines who we are. But we want that freedom back. From now on, we decide by ourselves.

process

During brainstorming, we presented our ideas in a mind map. Schizophrenia quickly came into focus. Starting from this word, our idea and the first storyboard emerged. In keeping with the theme, we wanted to go for a rather chilly, serious atmosphere. We created a mood board accordingly.
For the shoot, we were able to use a room in an office with a balcony belonging to it. We transported the equipment already on Thursday evening so that we could start shooting immediately on Friday. Thanks to the experienced actresses were able to realize our ideas very well according to our wishes.
